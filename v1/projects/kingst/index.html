King Street Dog Walking

branding / web / print

Skills: Photoshop, HTML, SCSS, PHP

Website: www.kingstdogwalking.com

Github: kingstdogwalking

King St. Dog Walking is an independent dog walking firm in Alexandria, Virginia. As a family owned business, they needed a website to help market and grow the business, a logo, business cards and several print materials.

This is a static website with a few pages built with HTML5, CSS3 and a PHP driven contact form that delivers an email to several inboxes.
