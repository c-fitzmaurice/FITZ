branding / print

Skills: Photoshop, Graphic Design

Dribbble: Dayton Virginia Town Logo

The historic town of Dayton, Virginia is located in Northern Virginia and is the oldest settled counties in the state. In 2015 they are looking to revitalize their downtown distric, starting with new branding and banners to diplay in their charming downtown distric.

From pictures provided to me, we choose a flat design of local landmarks to bring the town into a modern light while holding onto their historic roots.
